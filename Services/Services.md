# Services

## Consulting & IT Management

With hands-on experience across offensive security and IT operations, I offer a range of consulting and managed services designed to help businesses strengthen their security posture and optimise their technology environments.

---

### Penetration Testing

Identify vulnerabilities before adversaries do. I conduct thorough penetration tests across networks, web applications, and infrastructure to provide actionable findings and prioritised remediation guidance. Engagements are scoped to your environment and aligned with industry frameworks such as OWASP and PTES.

### IT Asset Discovery

Gain full visibility into your environment. Using active and passive discovery techniques, I help organisations build a complete and accurate inventory of devices, services, and software across their network -- including assets that may have gone untracked or forgotten.

### IT Asset Management

Knowing what you have is only the first step. I assist with establishing and maintaining asset management processes, including lifecycle tracking, ownership assignment, and integration with existing ITSM tooling to ensure your inventory stays current and audit-ready.

### Architecture & Design Consulting

Whether you are standing up new infrastructure, migrating to the cloud, or re-evaluating your current topology, I provide architecture and design guidance grounded in security-first principles. Services include network design review, segmentation planning, and technology selection aligned with your operational requirements and risk appetite.
